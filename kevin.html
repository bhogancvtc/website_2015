About me

Hi all, I am Kevin Young and this is my agile web page. I am a student at CVTC taking the Information Technology - Software Developer course. I live in Eau Claire and am married. My wife is from the Philippines and going to meet her for the first time was an interesting experience. We have a terrific little boy who is 4 years old.

Along with the Agile class I am also taking the Capstone. This is a course to create a final project working with a team. Last year while watching my son play a memory game on the computer I had an idea for a program. At the time I did not feel skilled enough to make it. At the start of the Capstone class we were asked to list ideas for projects to work on. To my surprise four other students liked the idea. Unfortunately that made our group to big so one had to take a different project.

There are several parts to this idea. A memory game, a math game, and a language game. At this time we chose to focus on the memory game. The idea was to create a memory game that people could add their own images to along with a caption. Then when their child played the game they would be able to see an image and start relating the caption to that image. One of the other members had a great idea to add to that. When a match is made a message would pop-up saying "Congratulations you found " then the caption would appear one letter at a time.
